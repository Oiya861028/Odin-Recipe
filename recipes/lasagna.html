Lasagna

The best lasagna you'll ever taste

Lasagna is a delicious and hearty dish that originated in Italy. fIt consists of layers of pasta sheets, cheese, meat sauce, and sometimes vegetables or béchamel sauce. The pasta sheets are usually boiled before assembling the dish, but some recipes use no-boil noodles that cook in the oven. The cheese is typically a mixture of ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan, but other varieties can be used as well. The meat sauce is made with ground beef, sausage, onion, garlic, tomato products, and various herbs and spices. The dish is then baked until bubbly and golden on top. Lasagna is a great meal for feeding a crowd or freezing for later. It can be served with salad, bread, or on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Procedures

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook all the meats in one pan, add all the seasonings. Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours
  3. Mix and stir in tomatos and water
  4. Boil and slightly salt some water in a pot. Cook lasagna noodle for 8 to 10 minutes. Rinse with cold water
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine cheese and egg, the remaining parsley, and salt
  6. Preheat the Oven to 375 degrees F
  7. In a baking dish, assemble in the following order:
    1. One layer of meat sauce
    2. One layer of noddles
    3. Riccota Mixtures
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmasan Cheese
    7. Repeat. Top with remaining parmasan
