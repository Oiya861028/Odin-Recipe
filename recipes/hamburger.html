Hamburger

A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a patty of ground beef served within a split bread roll, with various garnishes such as lettuce, onion, tomato, and sliced cucumber pickle, along with condiments like mustard, mayonnaise, ketchup and other saucesÂ¹. The patty itself is often seasoned or augmented with chopped onions, spices or bread crumbs before cooking. A slice of cheese is melted over the patty in the variation known as the cheeseburger.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • BBQ sauce
  • Butter
  • Onion(optional)

Procedures

  1. Kneed the Ground Beef into a ball. Press down to flatten it. **Important don't flatten too much, leave half a inch of thickness**
  2. Salt and pepper the patty on both side
  3. Mix the sauce in the ratio of 2 ketchup to 1 BBQ and Mayonnaise
  4. Heat the pan. Drop 1 tablespoon of butter for every bun. Toast the bun for 1 minute
  5. Oil the pan and put two patties in. Flip constantly for 3 minute. Then drop a stick of butter in and baste the patty for another minute. Last, place a slice of cheese on top of the patty, turn off the fire, and cover the pan for 30 second.
  6. Assemble the burger:
    1. Sauce the bottom bun
    2. Add a slice of lettuce and onion on top
    3. Add the patty
    4. Sauce the top bun and finish the burger
